Eric Carle is the author and illustrator of more than
70 books for very young children including his most
well-known title:
The Very Hungry Caterpillar!
Shel Silverstein authored and illustrated many books for children
including The Giving Tree, A Light in the Attic, The Missing Piece,
Everything On It, and Where the Sidewalk Ends.
Beatrix Potter was an author and illustrator from London,
England. She is best known for her 23 children’s books, including
The Tale of Peter Rabbit, The Tale of Jemima Puddle-Duck,
The Tale of Benjamin Bunny, and many more!
Don Freeman was an illustrator and writer of many children’s books.
One of his most well known books is called Corduroy. Corduroy is a
stuffed bear in search of his missing button. This book was so well loved,
that many more books about Corduroy were created.
Talitha Shipman is a newer illustrator of picture books for children.
Her favorite subjects to paint are wild kids and wild animals. Nature
inspires Talitha’s painting, and she hopes her work encourages curiosity
and creativity in children of all ages.
